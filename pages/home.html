Our Features

Outfit Recomendations

With just one click, the app suggests an outfit based on the current weather conditions. This feature ensures you’re always dressed appropriately for the day. If you really like the suggestion, you can save it to your favorites. No more guessing what to wear each morning!

Virtual Try-On

If you’re unsure about how the suggested outfit will look on you, use the virtual try-on feature. This allows you to see a digital representation of the outfit on yourself. Make confident decisions without any doubt! Try before you wear, all from the comfort of your home.

Categorizing Wardrobe

Even if your physical closet is messy, Wardrobe Wiz keeps your digital wardrobe organized. It categorizes your clothes into tops, bottoms, and dresses for easy access. Enjoy a clutter-free and organized wardrobe experience! Say goodbye to the hassle of finding clothes!

Lookbook

All suggested outfits are automatically saved in the Lookbook feature. This serves as a reference for future outfit ideas and inspiration. Easily revisit and reuse your favorite looks anytime! Keep your fashion inspirations at your fingertips!
